The Camp

Come take a crash course in competitive Overwatch! Based in sunny Irvin, California, the home of Blizzard Entertainment, we offer an amazing week long camp for anyone looking to improve their competitive skills. Whether you're brand new to the game or an experienced salt miner, you'll be sure to learn here.
