Rebuilding My PC

As a fun project this summer, I decided to rebuild my home PC with a new CPU and graphics card. My old CPU was an Intel i5 chip so my selection of the new AMD Ryzen chip meant that I also had to get a new motherboard, RAM and a new case to fit everything. It was a lot of fun and I got to learn a bunch about CPU overclocking and AIO water cooling solutions. I may have had too much fun with the RGB lighting though...
